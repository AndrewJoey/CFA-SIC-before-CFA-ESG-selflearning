# Part 1- introduction

Which of the following best defines "sustainable investment"?
纠错收藏
A
Investing only in assets of environmental protection industries

B
An investment method that pursues financial returns while taking into account environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors

C
Investments that abandon financial returns and prioritize social value

D
Investing only in companies with an ESG rating of AAA

【单选题】
正确答案：B
你的答案：

考点：1.可持续投资导论

答案解析： The core of sustainable investment is the "dual goal", which is to balance financial returns and ESG value. It does not mean abandoning financial returns or being limited to specific industries/ratings. A, C, and D are all one-sided: A limits the industry, C negates financial returns, and D limits the rating, all of which do not conform to the core definition of sustainable investment.
